Core Features

Direct buffer manipulation for real-time organic modeling.

Standard Brush

Displaces geometry along vertex normals to add volume and form instantly. The fundamental tool for blocking out shapes.

Smooth & Flatten

Apply Laplacian smoothing to relax topology or calculate average planes to create hard-surface planer effects.

Vertex Painting

Cubic-falloff airbrush system. Apply vertex colors with smooth gradients and opacity layering directly onto the mesh.

History Stack

Comprehensive Undo/Redo system capturing geometry and color buffers ensures data integrity during your workflow.

OBJ Export

Native generation of Wavefront .obj files for seamless interoperability with Blender, Maya, and ZBrush.

PBR Materials

Real-time switching between Standard, Clay (Rough), and Metallic (Specular) materials to inspect surface details.
